About Us

Govt. College of Technology started its DAE classes in 2016. It is 2 KM away from Shamshabad situated at Farooq-e-Azam Road near Sadiqabad Police Station. It has total area of 11 kanals comprising of admin block, academic block, library, labs, multipurpose hall and canteen etc.
